Logger example

This example runs a timer at 1kHz and logs the time and different variables at 100 Hz to a file, and then at 300 Hz to a second file.

The output files are created in the build/examples/03-logger folder and are called log1_at_100_Hz.csv and log2_at_300_Hz.csv
